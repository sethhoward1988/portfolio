about me

Hello. My name is Kitsudar (it's Thai in case you're wondering), I go by "Kit" for short too. I'm a fulltime/freelance designer based in Denver, Colorado. By day I work as a designer at RTD, a public transportation company doing creative, award-winning marketing campaigns. By (early) night, I hang out with my twin toddlers and husband. When all is quiet, I get to crank my creative juices on personal or freelance projects.

I'm always looking for new creative opportunities, including pro bono/non-profit. Please drop me a line to get in touch or just to say "Hi".
